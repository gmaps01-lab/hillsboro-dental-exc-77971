Frequently Asked Questions About Sleep Apnea Dentist

How much does sleep apnea treatment with a dental appliance typically cost at Hillsboro Dental Excellence?
At Hillsboro Dental Excellence, the cost for a custom dental appliance to treat sleep apnea generally ranges from $2,000 to $3,500. This price includes your initial consultation, custom fitting, appliance fabrication, and follow-up adjustments. Many medical insurance plans in Oregon may cover part of the treatment if you have a documented sleep apnea diagnosis. Our team is happy to help check your specific insurance benefits and discuss payment plan options.
How long does it take to get fitted for a sleep apnea appliance and start treatment?
The process typically takes about three to four weeks from your initial consultation to receiving your custom dental appliance. At your first visit, we’ll review your sleep study results and take digital impressions of your teeth. Your appliance is then fabricated at a specialized dental lab. Once it arrives (usually within two to three weeks), you’ll return for a fitting and adjustment. Most patients in Hillsboro can begin using their appliance within a month of starting the process.
Is Dr. Skinner at Hillsboro Dental Excellence licensed and qualified to provide sleep apnea dental devices?
Yes, Dr. Skinner is a licensed dentist in Oregon with advanced training in dental sleep medicine. He is a member of the American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine and regularly completes continuing education on the latest sleep apnea treatments. Our practice follows all Oregon state and federal guidelines for dental appliance therapy for sleep apnea.
What is the process for getting a dental appliance for sleep apnea at your Hillsboro office?
First, you’ll schedule an initial consultation, where we’ll review your sleep study (if you have one) and discuss symptoms. If you haven’t had a sleep study, we can refer you to a local sleep specialist. Next, we take digital scans and impressions of your teeth. Your custom appliance is crafted to fit your mouth comfortably and effectively. Once it’s ready, we’ll schedule a fitting appointment to ensure proper placement and make any needed adjustments. We also provide ongoing follow-up visits to monitor your progress and appliance fit.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on your sleep apnea dental appliances?
Yes, our dental sleep appliances come with a limited manufacturer’s warranty, typically covering defects for one to three years, depending on the appliance brand. Additionally, we offer free adjustments and follow-up care for the first year to ensure optimal comfort and effectiveness. If you have concerns about your appliance or its fit, our Hillsboro team is always available to help.
