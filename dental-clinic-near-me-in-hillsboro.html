What is the process for getting a sleep apnea oral appliance at Hillsboro Dental Excellence?
If you suspect sleep apnea, we start with a consultation to review your sleep history and may coordinate with your physician for a sleep study if you haven’t had one. Once diagnosed, we take digital impressions of your teeth to custom-fit your oral appliance. After fabrication (about 2–3 weeks), we’ll fit and adjust the device for comfort and efficacy. Follow-up visits ensure the appliance is working well and address any adjustments needed.
Do you guarantee results for Invisalign or sleep apnea treatment, and what if I’m not satisfied?
While individual results can vary, our office stands behind our care. For Invisalign, we offer complimentary refinement trays if your teeth need minor adjustments after your initial treatment (within a reasonable timeframe). For sleep apnea appliances, we provide follow-up visits to fine-tune your device and ensure it’s effective.
