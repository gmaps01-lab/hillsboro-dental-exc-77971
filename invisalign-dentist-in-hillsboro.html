Frequently Asked Questions About Invisalign Dentist

How long does Invisalign treatment usually take for adults in Hillsboro?
For most adult patients in the Hillsboro area, Invisalign treatment at our office typically takes between 9 and 18 months, though minor corrections can sometimes be completed in as little as 6 months. The exact timeline depends on your specific alignment needs and how consistently you wear your aligners. During your initial consultation, Dr. Skinner will use digital imaging to give you a customized treatment plan and a realistic estimate of how long your process will take.
Is Hillsboro Dental Excellence licensed and experienced in providing both Invisalign and sleep apnea dental services?
Yes, our lead dentist, Dr. Skinner, is a licensed Oregon dentist with advanced training in Invisalign and dental sleep medicine. You can trust our team's experience and ongoing education in both areas.
What is the process for starting Invisalign at your Hillsboro office?
The process begins with a complimentary consultation at our Hillsboro clinic, where we assess your teeth and discuss your goals. If you’re a good candidate, we’ll take 3D digital scans (no messy impressions needed) to create a precise treatment plan. After you approve your plan, your custom aligners are made and typically ready within 2-3 weeks. We’ll schedule a fitting appointment, review how to wear and care for your aligners, and set you up with periodic check-ins every 6-8 weeks to monitor your progress.
Do you guarantee results with Invisalign, and what happens if I’m not satisfied with my outcome?
At Hillsboro Dental Excellence, patient satisfaction is our priority. While every case is unique, we stand behind our Invisalign treatments. If your teeth haven’t shifted as planned at the end of your initial series of aligners, we offer complimentary refinement aligners to help achieve your desired results. Our team will work with you until you’re happy with your new smile, provided you’ve followed the treatment guidelines and attended your scheduled visits.
