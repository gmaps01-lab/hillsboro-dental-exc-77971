Frequently Asked Questions About Dentist

How much does Invisalign treatment typically cost at Hillsboro Dental Excellence, and does insurance cover any of it?
At Hillsboro Dental Excellence, Invisalign treatment costs generally range from $3,500 to $6,000, depending on the complexity of your case and the length of treatment. Many dental insurance plans in Oregon offer partial coverage for orthodontic treatments like Invisalign, but the amount covered can vary. Our team will verify your insurance benefits before you start and provide a detailed cost estimate, including any payment plan options we offer to make treatment more affordable.
How long does Invisalign treatment take, and how often will I need to visit your Hillsboro office?
Most Invisalign treatments at our Hillsboro practice take between 12 and 18 months, though milder cases can sometimes be completed in as little as six months. You’ll typically need to visit us every 6 to 8 weeks for progress checks and to receive your next set of aligners. We work to accommodate busy schedules and can provide evening or weekend appointments when possible.
Is Hillsboro Dental Excellence licensed to provide sleep apnea dental appliances, and what training do your dentists have?
Yes, our dentists are fully licensed in Oregon and have completed advanced training in dental sleep medicine. They are qualified to diagnose obstructive sleep apnea in collaboration with your physician and to custom-fit oral appliances. Dr. Skinner, our lead dentist, is a member of the American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine and regularly attends continuing education courses to stay current with the latest treatment protocols.
What is the process for getting a custom sleep apnea appliance at your Hillsboro office?
The process starts with an initial consultation, where we review your medical and sleep history and, if needed, coordinate with your primary care provider or sleep specialist. We then take digital scans of your teeth to design a custom oral appliance. Once the device is ready (usually in 2-3 weeks), you’ll return for a fitting and adjustments. We’ll schedule follow-up visits to ensure the appliance is comfortable and effective, and we’ll help you navigate any insurance paperwork.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on Invisalign or sleep apnea appliances?
We stand behind the quality of our work. Our Invisalign treatments include complimentary refinement aligners if minor adjustments are needed after your main treatment, as long as you follow the treatment plan. For sleep apnea appliances, most devices come with a 1- to 3-year manufacturer’s warranty against defects. If you experience issues with fit or comfort, we’ll work with you to resolve them promptly at our Hillsboro office.
